\subsection{Introduction}
\label{sec:plugins:SCM:Introduction}

The creation of skycultures (Chapter \ref{ch:SkyCultures}) with its many files used to be difficult. 
Some time ago, \citet{Zotti-Wolf:SEAC2019} mentioned shortcomings and problems that should be 
approached in a redesign of Stellarium's skycultures, developments that had to wait until 2025. 

This plugin finally helps with the creation of a new skyculture in the new format, 
in a user-friendly wizard-like editor that checks that all relevant parts are included in the right format.

\subsection{Further development}
The plugin and its description here are  still in development. 
It is mostly geared towards skyculture researchers who want to see their results publicised via Stellarium. 
Members of non-European cultures who are actually using the
output and can judge its correctness are invited to report errors or
suggest better formatting for their respective skycultures.
